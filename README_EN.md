## Install the render
Works on Android and iOS.

- Download the [CycloRoute.render.xml](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Hades1503/OsmAnd_Cycling_Map/main/CycloRoute.render.xml) file
  - **From a computer** : Right-click [this link](https://github.com/Hades1503/OsmAnd_Cycling_Map/raw/main/CycloRoute.render.xml) â†’ <kbd><samp>Save Link As...</samp></kbd>.
  - **From a smartphone** : Long-press [this link](https://github.com/Hades1503/OsmAnd_Cycling_Map/raw/main/CycloRoute.render.xml) â†’ <kbd><samp>Download link</samp></kbd>.<br>
    <br>
    *Tutorial made under <a href="https://www.mozilla.org/fr/firefox/new/">Firefox</a> browser â€“ words used might be different with another.*<br>
    <br>
- Open your downloaded file, then choose `Open with OsmAnd`. Your style went imported!
- Open OsmAnd, then on the left sidemenu, press `Configure map`
- Scroll down to `Map style`
- Pick `Hiking`, your new rendering.
- Finished, enjoy! ðŸŽ‰
